Problems with Extracurricular Activities in students' lives

What are extracurricular activities?

Extracurricular activities are activities, performed by students,
that falls outside the realm of the normal curriculum of school, college or university education.
Such activities are generally voluntary (as opposed to mandatory), social, philanthropic,
and often involve others of the same age.

The main problem here is that school can take up too much of your time. Having to wake up early, do a ton of work,
arrive home late and do more work every day gets exhausting and students often dont have enough energy for anything else.
Another problem could be money and time, not everyone can afford to spend a lot of money and time is also an issue considering
that students need to do homework, study, and also hang out with friends. There is also the feeling that you started doing an activity
too late. If you want to start something, but you see someone much younger than you doing a lot better, it may make you lose confidence
or even quit

Of course, taking extracurricular courses is a good oportunity to get into university and if someone has all the right
they should go, but at the same time not everyone can do that.
